About the Project

I want to make a tiny house that is accessible and afforable as possible. And a variety of possibility requires a variety of help and skills.

Can you help me?

To love and be loved as I have been is a gift.

I think it's kind of weird to name a house but it seemed like this project should have some reference than just "My Tiny House Project". So, out of my love for alliteration, Sweet Spirit came up.

Sweet from sweetgrass inspired from my long journey of completing Braiding Sweetgrass: Indigenous Wisdom, Scientific Knowledge, and the Teachings of Plants by Robin Wall Kimmerer. And Spirit from Two-Spirit. More info here (eventually).

Braiding Sweetgrass has has been a book that so succinctly reflects my understanding and belief of gratitude and I really appreciate that. Being Two-Spirit reflects me.

This project is to give. Give back? I want to love people just how they are, how the see fit, in their own worlds.

I've also wrestled with Black De Novo. Because I'm Black and that holds significance. And I learned 'de novo' from Jessica Jones Season 2. It means to begin a new. Or from what I understood from the show: to begin again; belonging nowhere. Which is how I often feel and seems to fit with the experience of traveling.

What do you think?
