About Me

I don’t believe that it is necessary for me to talk about myself when this work should be valued and worthwhile and getting done whether or not it is me doing it. I’m also not inclined to talk about myself and when I do it usually isn’t well. However, it is my understanding that in order for people to be persuaded to participate in something they must find interest in it, and in many cases they must find themselves in it through someone or something they can relate to. Given that I need help and all kinds of support, it seems appropriate and necessary that I do what it takes to get folks interested including allowing people to get to know me. As a compromise, I have provided a list of stories or testimonials from people who know and have known me, so that you (someone who I hope may support this work in some capacity) may become familiar with me and why I can do this project. Check out a few below.
