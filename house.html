The House

  • The thing I’ll live in
  • A tool that can feed the tongue and the tummy; sharing stories and food.

The house is something I will build by hand and make with community. It is something I will share with the people who welcome me into their lives for a moment over food and stories. I intend it to be a space that is as accessible as possible for people who use power and manual wheelchairs and those with auditory issues and light sensitivities. There are several steps and processes involved to complete the house. The first steps being getting a truck and finding a build site! The following steps are listed below.

  1. Find building site (CA: Bay Area or LA Area)
  2. Purchasing and transporting trailer to site
  3. Gathering and transporting foundational materials
    • Timber frame
    • SIPS
    • Roofing
  4. Gathering and transporting heating, cooling, water and power materials
    • Solar panels and monitors
    • Electric heater?
    • Water heater?
    • Passive?
    • Lighting
  5. Internal fixtures
    • Cabinetry & counters, sink & stove
    • Wall mounted table
    • bookshelf
    • Etc
  6. Furniture and what not

knowledge sharing on construction, design, maintenance, solar engineering, plumbing, woodworking, other things I haven’t considered
