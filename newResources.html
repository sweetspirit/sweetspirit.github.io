Frequently Asked Questions

    What motivates you to do this?

    Much of my reasoning is listed on the about page. For more background on those subject matters, check out these resources (Link).

    Generally, my desire is to meet people, hear stories, learn about food/plants/medicines, and feed people in the process. I want to learn about the myriad foodways of Black and Native peoples of this land mass we refer to as the United States. I want to love people just how they are, how the see fit, in their own worlds.

    Why the name Sweet Spirit?

    I think it's kind of weird to name a house but it seems to be a common practice and this project should have some reference other than just "My Tiny House Project". So, out of my love for alliteration, I thought of Sweet Spirit.

    Sweet from sweetgrass inspired by my long journey of completing Braiding Sweetgrass: Indigenous Wisdom, Scientific Knowledge, and the Teachings of Plants by Robin Wall Kimmerer. Braiding Sweetgrass has been a book that so succinctly reflects my thoughts around gratitude and I really appreciate that. And Spirit from Two-Spirit (link) is me. In summary, the name casts who I am at the core into the light, but I need help to grow; I cannot do it on my own. So, I'm asking for help.
