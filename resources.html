Frequently Asked Questions



    What movtivates you to do this?

    Much of my reasoning is listed on the about page. For more background on those subject matters, check out these resources (Link).

    Generally, my desire is to meet people, hear stories, learn about food/plants/medicines, and feed people in the process. I want to learn about the myriad foodways of Black and Native peoples of this land mass we refer to as the United States. I want to love people just how they are, how the see fit, in their own worlds.

    Why Sweet Spirit?

    I think it's kind of weird to name a house but it seemed like this project should have some reference than just "My Tiny House Project". So, out of my love for alliteration, Sweet Spirit came up.

    Sweet from sweetgrass inspired from my long journey of completing Braiding Sweetgrass: Indigenous Wisdom, Scientific Knowledge, and the Teachings of Plants by Robin Wall Kimmerer. And Spirit from Two-Spirit (link). In summary, a healer is who I am but I need help to grow; I cannot do it on my own.

    Braiding Sweetgrass has has been a book that so succinctly reflects my understanding and belief of gratitude and I really appreciate that. Being Two-Spirit reflects me.

    I've also thought about Black De Novo after watching Jessica Jones. Because I'm Black and that holds significance. And I learned 'de novo' from Jessica Jones Season 2. It means to begin a new. Or from what I understood from the show: to begin again; belonging nowhere. Which is how I often feel and seems to fit with the experience of traveling.
